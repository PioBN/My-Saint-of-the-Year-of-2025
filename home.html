My Saint of the Year of 2025

In Christianity, a Saint is someone who is holy and dedicated to God. Saints are people who live good lives and follow God's teachings. They love others, help those in need, and remain faithful to God even in hard times. Some Saints are well-known for performing miracles through God's power. They can also do miracles for us if we pray and ask for their help, and they can intercede for us by praying to God on our behalf. Christians look up to Saints as examples of how to live in a way that pleases God.
